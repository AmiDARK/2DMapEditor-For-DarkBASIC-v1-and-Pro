AMBIENT LIGHT :

The following affects ambient light .

That command allow user to change color and intensity of ambient light .

Keyboards : Left and right arrows to modify Red component. , Up and Down arrows to modify Green component and PageUP/PageDown For the blue component. Inser/Suppr affect light intensity .

That icon can change the Ambient light orientation .

Les touches : Left and right arrows to modify X orientation. , Up and Down arrows to modify Yorientation and PageUP/PageDown For Zorientation.Values X=0 , Y=0 , Z=0 will force ambient light to apply with the same intensity to all faces of all objects in map..
